Talk presented at **ROSCon 2025 in Singapore** about open-source robotics observability at scale with COS (Canonical Observability Stack) for devices.

COS for robotics brings observability to robots and devices, offering an infrastructure to collect, monitor, and visualize data from a fleet of devices using snaps and Juju.
